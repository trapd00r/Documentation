This document is a reproduction of a book issued by Digital in 1983. It contains details of terminal and printer equipment, such as the VT100 and VT200 family of terminals, teleprinters, band printers and the LN01 laser printer. All of these products are obsolete, but may still be in service around the world. This document contains many valuable details about operating and programming this equipment.

I have attempted to contact Digital (now Compaq) about the copyright of this book but, as you might expect, have been unable to talk to anyone who could even remember what a terminal was. More details in the Production Notes.
