Chapter 9

Introduction: Printing Terminals and Printers

Introduction

Digital offers a variety of quality printing terminals and printers. Table 9-1 summarizes the many features of Digital's KSR and RO printers, while Table 9-2 summarizes the key features of Digital's system printers. Further details on these products can be found in the following chapters and appendices.

Chapters

Chapters 10 through 17 describe the following products.

  • Chapter 10, Letterprinter 100/Letterwriter 100 (LA100) – Discusses similarities and differences between these products.
  • Chapter 11, LQP02 Letter-Quality Printer
  • Chapter 12, LA50 Personal Printer
  • Chapter 13, DECwriter Correspondent (LA12)
  • Chapter 14, DECwriter III/DECprinter III (LA120) – Discusses similarities and differences between these products.
  • Chapter 15, LN01 Laser Printer
  • Chapter 16, LP25, LP26 and LP27 System Printers – Discusses the similarities and differences of these products.
  • Chapter 17, LXY Family of Graphics Lineprinters – Discusses the similarities and differences between these products.
